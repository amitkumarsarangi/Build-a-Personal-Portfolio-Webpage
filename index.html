DevAstra.IN
Warm greetings from Amit and thanks a lot for stopping by this site. This website is an expression of my digital side and an open ground for showcasing my acumen in various web technologies. I cannot express enough how much I enjoy learning web technologies and languages like Python, JS, PHP, Sass, Angular, MySql and others. I am a JavaScript zealot and a JSON enthusiast. Solving coding challenges is a nice way for passing the time. And when I am not busy coding, I am playing this Game about Squares.
DevAstra.IN is just in its initial phase of development. Slowly this site will evolve into something worthy and useful for newbie coders. But what is in this name - DevAstra, you must be wondering? DevAstra is simply Dev and Astra. You may pronounce it as you like. We all know that 'Dev' is a short form of developer or development. For those who admire Linux understand that 'Dev' (more precisely /dev) is the location where all the device files are located. 'Astra' was the best thing I could think of that must follow 'Dev'. While in Greek 'Astra' (or άστρα) means 'Stars'; in Sanskrit, 'Astra' (or अस्त्र) refers to celestial weapons. I hope you get the thought :-)
